About Wake N Bake

Our adventure started with one humble shop in Nashik, Maharashtra and developed to become most sought out recipes under the moving vision of our author, Tejas Bhadane in the mid 2019. From that point forward, our family has joined their long periods of involvement with the vision of a network of cooperative shop proprietors, providers and different accomplices cooperating to make a brand name in the market to develop their business to what it is today. We bake happiness for you in the face of cake. Let us deliver sweetness in your lives.
